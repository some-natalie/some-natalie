---
title: "Infused bourbons"
excerpt: "What to do when you try one you don't like 🥃"
---

> Adding flavorful ingredients to steep in bourbon can change a bottle you're not thrilled with into something delicious.  Give each a good 3-6 months to steep!

3 cups is just a guess - most are scaled to about what's left of a 750 mL bottle once you've sampled enough to decide you don't like it as is. 🥃

### Apple pie

- 2 granny smith apples, sliced
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 2 vanilla beans, split
- 0.5 teaspoon whole cloves
- 3 cups bourbon whiskey

### Cherry bounce

- 2 pounds (about 4 cups) fresh sweet dark cherries, pits removed
- 3 cups bourbon whiskey
- 0.5 cup caster sugar

### Orange vanilla

- 2 oranges, peels only
- 2 whole vanilla beans
- 3 cups bourbon whiskey

### Peach pecan

- 4 peaches, peeled and cut into chunks
- 1 cup raw pecans
- 3 cups bourbon whiskey

### Vanilla chai

- 3 chai tea bags
- 2 vanilla beans, split
- 3 cups bourbon whiskey
