> Everyone’s favorite way to use up brown bananas

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 0.5 cup white whole wheat flour
- 0.67 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup rolled oats
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 0.25 teaspoon baking soda
- 0.25 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
- 0.25 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 overripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 0.33 cup whole buttermilk
- 0.25 cup vegetable oil
- 1.5 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Grease a loaf pan and set aside.
1. Put all ingredients in mixing bowl, stir with stand mixer until smooth.
1. Pour batter in loaf pan - should be about half-ish full.
1. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
1. Cool before cutting.

## Notes

- Frozen bananas are fine, but bring to room temperature first.
