> One more way to use up brown bananas - it's pancakes!

## Ingredients

- 2 very ripe large bananas
- 1 cup milk (preferably whole milk)
- 1 large egg
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 0.25 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 0.25 teaspoon ground clove
- 0.25 teaspoon kosher salt
- Butter or canola oil spray for the skillet

### Toppings or mix-ins

- maple syrup
- chopped walnuts or pecans
- sliced banana
- chocolate chips
- whipped cream

## Instructions

1. Smash the bananas in a big bowl until smooth.
1. Add the rest of the wet ingredients and whisk until well combined.
1. Add the dry ingredients - flour, sugar, baking powder, and seasonings.  Stir until smooth and uniform.
1. Stir in nuts or chocolate chips, if using.
1. Cook, using roughly a quarter cup of batter to each pancake.

## Notes

- Cooked pancakes keep well for a few days, freeze well
- Batter lends itself to small pancakes
