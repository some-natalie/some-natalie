### North Carolina vinegar sauce

- 1 quart cider vinegar
- 0.33 cup crushed red pepper
- 1.5 teaspoons black pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt

Use less crushed red pepper to turn down the heat.

### Kentucky bourbon sauce

- 2 cups tomato sauce
- 0.5 cup sugarcane molasses
- 0.5 cup apple cider vinegar
- 0.5 cup bourbon
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

### Alabama white sauce

- 2 cups mayonnaise (real mayo, no fake stuff)
- 1 cup distilled white vinegar
- 0.5 cup apple juice
- 2 tsp prepared horseradish
- 2 tsp ground black pepper
- 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp cayenne pepper

No cook - just mix and let sit!

### South Carolina mustard sauce

- 0.75 cup yellow mustard
- 0.5 cup honey
- 0.25 apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon hot sauce

### Tonkatsu sauce (Japanese BBQ)

- 0.33 cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon mirin
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 0.25 teaspoon garlic powder
