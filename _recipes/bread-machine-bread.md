> Whole wheat bread for the bread machine

## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 3.5 tbsp sugar
- 0.25 tsp salt, rounded scoop
- 1 tbsp butter
- 0.67 cup milk
- 1 medium egg
- 1 tsp active dry yeast

## Directions

1. Mix everything in except the yeast.  If the bread machine has a hopper, put it in there.  If not, make a well of dry flour for the yeast if not starting the bread immediately.
1. Medium loaf settings (yields ~1 lb loaf), basic bake with the light crust setting.

## Notes

- May need to stop ten minutes early on some machines.
