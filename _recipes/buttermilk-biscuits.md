> Fast whole-grain drop biscuits.  Pair with [sausage gravy](../recipes/sausage-gravy) for breakfast 🤤

## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
- 0.5 cup whole wheat flour (red or white)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 0.5 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 tablespoon cold butter, lard, or other rendered fat
- 0.67 cup whole fat buttermilk

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl
1. Cut in the cold butter until you have pea-sized butter chunks (Or, try grating frozen butter with a cheese grater and adding the shreds to the flour)
1. Add just enough buttermilk to make a heavy, wet dough
1. "Knead" the dough lightly until it sticks together
1. Pat dough out on a well-floured surface to approximately one inch thick _or_ drop it by large spoonful onto an ungreased cookie sheet
1. Bake in preheated 425 degree oven approximately 10 minutes, and cool on a wire rack

## Notes

- Use cold fat! This is important to ensure that you end up with a nice, flaky biscuit.
- Do not over-knead. The heat of your hands will cause the fat to warm up - this makes the biscuits tough.
- Do not over-bake - they get kind of gummy.
