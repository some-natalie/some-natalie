> Shortcut to lots of flavors in anything you make with butter 🧈

### Orange honey

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 tbsp honey
* 2 tsp finely grated orange zest

### Chives

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 0.25 cups of finely chopped fresh chives

### Fresh herbs

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 2 tbsp freshly chopped herbs (thyme, rosemary, parsley, etc)
* 0.5 tsp salt
* 0.5 tsp ground pepper

### Cinnamon maple

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
* 0.75 tsp ground cinnamon

### Jerk

* 1 tablespoon roasted and chopped garlic
* 2 to 3 teaspoons cayenne pepper
* 2 teaspoons onion powder
* 2 teaspoons thyme
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 2 teaspoons sugar
* 1 teaspoon ground allspice
* 1 teaspoon cumin
* 1 teaspoon paprika
* 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
* 0.25 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 0.5 teaspoon ground nutmeg
* 0.5 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
* 0.5 pound softened butter (2 sticks)

### Paprika

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, minced
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 0.5 tsp salt

### Harissa

* 0.5 stick of unsalted butter
* 0.25 cup fresh mint, chopped
* 3 tbsp harissa paste
* 1 tsp salt

### Thai

* 0.5 stick of unsalted butter
* 0.25 cup chopped fresh basil (Thai basil if available)
* 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
* 2 serrano chiles, seeded and finely chopped
* 1 tsp lime zest
* 1 tsp salt

### Porcini mushroom

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 2 tbsp finely chopped dried porcini mushrooms
* 1 tbsp red wine
* Salt to taste

### Turkish

* 0.5 stick of unsalted butter
* 1.5 tsp hot smoked Spanish paprika
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1 tsp salt
* 0.5 tsp ground allspice

### Indian

* 0.5 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 0.5 tsp ground turmeric
* 0.5 tsp ground coriander
* 0.5 tsp black pepper
* 0.5 tsp ground cinnamon
* 0.25 tsp ground cardamom

### Chipotle lime

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 canned chipotle chile in adobo, finely chopped
* 1 tsp fresh lime zest
* Salt to taste

### Ramps

* 1.5 pounds ramps (trimmed, cleaned, blanched for 30s, and thinly sliced)
* 4 pounds unsalted butter, cut up and at room temperature
* 2 lemons, zested and juiced
* Salt and pepper to taste
* Thyme (optional)

### Steakhouse

* 1 stick of unsalted butter
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
* 1 tbsp minced garlic

### Hibachi garlic

* 2 sticks of butter
* Hella garlic, like 3-4 whole bulbs
* 0.5 cup whipping cream
* Soy sauce
