> Mmmmmm - a cup of tea ☕

Use nice loose leaf tea and adjust each to your liking.  Use freeze-dried fruits and dried herbs if you're looking for shelf-stability.

Most of these are discontinued or hard-to-find blends from commercial teas.

## Black teas

**Taste of Summer** (black tea)

* Strawberries
* Orange zest
* Marigold petals
* Rose petals

**Four Red Fruit** (black tea)

* Strawberries
* Cherries
* Raspberries
* Redcurrants

**Blackberry Sage** (black tea)

* Blackberry preserves
* Dried or fresh sage leaves

**Apple Pie** (black tea)

* Apple cider concentrate (minimal pulp)
* Cinnamon

**Lady Gray** (black tea)

* Bergamot
* Lemon
* Orange
* Cornflowers

**Mary Twining Spice** (black tea)

* Candied ginger
* Cinnamon

**Orange Spice** (black tea)

* Orange zest and extract
* Cinnamon
* Clove

**Autumn Cranberry** (black tea)

* Hibiscus
* Dried cranberry
* Orange zest

**Capri** (black tea)

* Dried fig
* Lemon zest
* Vanilla extract (just a hint)

**Celebration** (black tea)

* Apricot
* Hazelnut
* Cinnamon
* Cornflowers

**Citrus** (black tea)

* Bergamot
* Grapefruit zest
* Orange zest

**Fruits d'Alsace** (black tea)

* Apricots
* Cherries
* Plums

**Strawberry Basil** (black tea)

* Strawberry puree (or jam)
* Fresh sweet basil
* Honey or sugar to taste

**Christmas Spice** (black tea)

* Clove
* Almond extract
* Orange zest
* Vanilla extract
* Cinnamon

**Chai** (Assam black tea)

* Cardamom seeds
* Cinnamon
* Nutmeg
* Clove
* Vanilla bean

**Paris** (black and oolong)

* Black currants
* Vanilla extract
* Bergamot oil
* Toasted sugar

## Green teas

**Caribe** (green tea)

* Hibiscus
* Guava
* Coconut
* Strawberry
* Marigold petals
* Cornflowers

**Zen Mint** (gunpowder green tea)

* Fresh mint (preferably spearmint)
* Sugar

**Jasmine** (gunpowder green tea)

* Jasmine flowers
* Honey

## Herbal teas

**Chamomile** (herbal)

* 2 parts chamomile
* 1 part borage flowers
* 1 part lemon balm
* 0.5 part lavender
* 0.5 part rose petals
* 0.25 part passion flower petals

**Gol Gav Zaban** (herbal)

* 2 tablespoons borage flower (gol gav zaban)
* 0.25 teaspoons saffron dissolved in 1 tablespoon boiling water
* 2 rock candy (Nabat), or sugar
* 1 teaspoon lemon juice

**Lactation Support** (herbal)

* 0.25 cup fenugreek seed
* 0.5 cup dried nettle leaf
* 0.5 cup dried red raspberry leaf
* 0.25 cup fennel seed
* 0.5 cup dried lemon verbena
* 0.25 cup dried blessed thistle
