> Shelf-stable cookies for feeding the entire scouting troop for a whole trip.  It's chocolate, peanut butter, and oatmeal measured by the pound.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 4 cups sugar
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 12 eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon corn syrup or honey
- 8 teaspoons baking soda
- 3 pounds peanut butter
- 18 cups rolled oats
- 1 pound chocolate chips (3 cups)
- 1 pound M&Ms® plain chocolate candy (3 cups)

## Directions

1. In large bowl combine all ingredients, mix until well blended.
1. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
1. Bake at 350°F. for 12 minutes (or so).

## Notes

- This recipe is all about packing a ton of calories in a shelf-stable format for all day outdoor activities.
