> It's pizza sauce. 🍕

## Ingredients

- 3 (15 oz) cans of tomato sauce
- Half of a white onion, finely chopped (about a rounded half cup)
- 4 or 5 cloves of garlic, finely diced (or about 2 tbsp of minced garlic)
- 1.5 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- A little bit of olive oil, maybe 2 tbsp
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- A few turns of black pepper from the mill (maybe half a teaspoon)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 225° F (about 110° C).
1. Combine all ingredients in a dutch oven.
1. Bake with the lid slightly cracked for 4-6 hours.
1. Remove and use for pizza, dipping bread, etc.

## Notes

- Freezes beautifully for making a big batch and then having individual pizza sized frozen bags.
- About 1 cup of sauce makes one large pizza.
