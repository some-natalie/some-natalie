> The mandatory Thanksgiving pumpkin pie 🥧

## Ingredients

- 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree (not the pie mix)
- 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
- 2 eggs
- 0.75 cup packed light brown sugar 
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp ground nutmeg
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp ground cloves
- 3 tablespoons bourbon
- 1 unbaked 9-inch pie crust

## Directions

1. Mix together until smooth.
1. Pour into pie shell.
1. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 350°F and bake for 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
1. Cool on a rack, serve warm or cool.

## Notes

- More or less the same recipe that's on the back of the pumpkin puree can.
