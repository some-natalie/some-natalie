> Easy breakfasts made in advance, tons of variations.

## Ingredients

- 1 pie crust
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup half and half
- 1 cup cheese, shredded or crumbled
- (mix ins, see notes)
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Mix 4 eggs, half and half, and salt and pepper.  Set aside.
1. Put cheese and any other mix-ins into pie crust.
1. Pour egg mixture into pie crust.
1. Bake 40-50 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean.

## Notes

- No need to be picky about the pie crust, pre-made is fine.  Non-flaky crusts work well too.
- Variations include
  - bacon, breakfast sausage, or chorizo
  - spinach and feta
  - everything bagel seasoning and cream cheese (instead of shredded cheese)
  - no meat, extra cheese!
  - fresh spring herbs (chives, chervil, parsley, tarragon, etc.)
  - caramelized onions
  - fresh onions and bell peppers
