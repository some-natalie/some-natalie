> Warm fall spices in a rich buttermilk cake

## Ingredients

- 1 cups brown sugar
- 1 stick butter, softened
- 0.5 cup vegetable oil
- 5 large eggs, separated
- 2 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp nutmeg
- 0.25 tsp cloves
- 0.25 tsp allspice
- pinch of salt
- 1 cup buttermilk

## Directions

1. Whisk dry ingredients together
1. Add wet ingredients and stir to combine
1. Bake for 25 min in two greased 9 inch cake pans at 350.

## Notes

- Doesn't halve neatly due to the eggs, but makes a fabulous layer cake with a basic buttercream icing.
