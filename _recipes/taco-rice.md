> Taco rice from scratch

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 2 cups chicken or vegetable stock
- 0.25 cup finely chopped onion
- 0.25 cup finely chopped bell pepper
- 2 tsp minced garlic
- 2 tsp fat (rendered bacon fat or corn oil)
- Half of a 6 oz can of tomato paste
- 0.5 tsp cumin
- Pinch or two of ancho chili powder

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice.  Set aside.
1. Combine fat, onion, and bell pepper.  Cook on medium-high heat until both are nicely browned but not burnt.  Lower heat to medium.
1. Add the garlic and sauté for a minute or so.
1. Add the rice, stock, tomato paste, and seasonings.  Stir well.  Bring to a boil on high heat.
1. Cover, simmer on low heat until rice is tender and liquid is fully absorbed.  About 25 minutes.
1. Mix veggies back in, serve.

## Notes

- This adds no salt until (optionally) at the end.  Since this is usually added into a burrito or taco with other salty ingredients, it's easy to end up with a dish that's too salty.
